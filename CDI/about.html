About us

Since 1991, we have been manufacturing cost-effective, high-quality products for audio/video, medical, industrial, security, retail, telecom, embedded control, and defense applications for some of the leading companies within the US.

In line with our company goals, and with 7 expansions over the last 4 years alone, we have the space and ability to accommodate any of your engineering and manufacturing needs.

We have a team of experienced electrical and mechanical engineers, software developers, and PCB designers. We are experts in the areas of DSP designs, audio and video applications, low-power portable devices, and wireless technologies. Our software development expertise includes real-time embedded, device drivers, Linux, WinCE, TI DSP/BIOS, GUI development, and beyond.

We offer purchasing, BOM risk-assessment, kitting, manufacturing test development, prototype runs, and high-volume production. With a wide variety of products enables us to provide high-quality, trusted services. We take you from design to manufacturing and every step in-between.
